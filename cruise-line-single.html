Carnival Cruise Line was established in 1972 and is the world's largest cruise line. A Carnival cruise is all about creating a fun, memorable vacation and their "Fun Ships" were designed to keep the fun going. On a Carnival Cruise ship there is always so much to do. On deck there are pools, waterslides, golf, a Seaside Theater and activities galore to keep kids and adults of all ages entertained. Kids love Camp Carnival and for a little quiet time adults can relax at the 'Adult-Only' Serenity Retreat or be pampered in the Spa Carnival. Dining options are varied and include casual dining on deck, traditional dining, sushi and elegant dining in the Steakhouse Restaurant. Plus 24-hour stateroom service is always available. At night the fun really comes alive with a variety of venues including comedy clubs, piano bars, Broadway shows, night clubs and of course the casino. galore to keep kids and adults of all ages entertained.
